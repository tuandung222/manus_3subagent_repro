How it works

Your personal AI agent, now with a browser in its toolkit.

Connect Browser

Enable Manus Browser Operator in the Manus connector tab. A browser extension will be installed.

Grant Access

Manus will ask for your permission to access your browser. Grant access, and the agent takes over.

Autonomous Action

Manus works in your browser to complete the task, using your logged-in sessions to gather information
